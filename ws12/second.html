ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Olli, and I am delighted to invite you into my world of art. Since as far back as I can remember, making art has always been one of my greatest inspirations and joys. Each step in the creative process brings me immense satisfaction, from the very beginning when an idea first strikes until the finishing touches are put in place. I especially enjoy digital art because the boundaries are endless. I love using tools like Blender and TouchDesigner to bring my visions to life with incredible detail and flexibility. For me, art is more than just a hobby; it's a vital part of my life that keeps me motivated and connected to the world around me.

Outside of art, you can find me at the gym or in the kitchen, whipping up something delicious. These activities inspire me and keep the energy up. I'm a huge believer in continuous learning; thus, I'm always in a lookout for opportunities that make me grow both as a person and a professional. I invite you to view my portfolio to review some of my projects. If you would like to collaborate or simply wish to talk, don't hesitate to get in touch!
